# ElevenLabs Music Prompt for MSP 2.0

## Prompt

**Create an instrumental track that captures the essence of MSP 2.0 - Music Side Project Studio: a modern, empowering tool for independent musicians to create and distribute their music as podcast feeds with Lightning Network payments.**

**Musical Direction:**
- Genre: Electronic/Ambient with modern production elements
- Mood: Inspiring, creative, forward-thinking, and empowering
- Tempo: Moderate (100-120 BPM), steady and purposeful
- Energy: Building and uplifting, but not overwhelming

**Musical Elements:**
- Start with ambient, atmospheric pads that evoke a creative workspace
- Introduce subtle electronic beats that feel modern and tech-forward
- Layer in melodic elements (synthesizers or soft electric piano) that convey innovation and possibility
- Include subtle rhythmic elements that suggest collaboration and connection
- Build gradually to a mid-section that feels empowering and expansive
- Add subtle harmonic textures that suggest the Lightning Network's energy (sparkling, electric, but smooth)
- End with a sense of completion and forward momentum

**Emotional Tone:**
- Should feel like the soundtrack to a creative studio where independent artists are empowered
- Convey both the artistic/creative side and the technical/innovative aspects
- Balance between intimate (personal creative space) and expansive (global distribution)
- Modern, clean, and professional but warm and inviting
- Should inspire creativity and feel like a tool that unlocks potential

**Avoid:**
- Overly aggressive or heavy elements
- Traditional rock or acoustic instrumentation (keep it modern/electronic)
- Melancholic or dark tones
- Overly complex or chaotic arrangements

**Target Length:** 2-3 minutes
